A laser game where one can shoot lasers and needs to collect blocks into bucket.

MOUSE:
	scroll in and out to zoom in and out.
	right click and drag to pan the scene.
	click on baskets and laser platform and drag to move them.

KEYBOARD:
	'a' and 'd' to tilt the cannon up and down.
	's' and 'f' to move it up and down.
	'n' and 'm' can be used to increase/decrease the speed of blocks falling down.
	ctrl+left and ctrl+right to move the red bucket left and right.
	alt+left and alt+right to move the blue bucket left and right.
	spacebar to shoot the laser.
	left, right, up and down keys to pan left, pan right, zoom in and zoom out.

Shoot the black blocks and try not to hit other blocks.
The red and blue blocks must be collected in corresponding baskets.
